About

Hello, welcome to my online portfolio! Here you can see my wide range of artworks and digital media I have produced over the years. Works

Digital Art

"Faded Memory"
Procreate
This piece aims to capture the feeling of an old photograph; something distant but familiar. I created this because to me, nostalgia is one of the best feelings and I hope to invoke it with this drawing.

"Stalker"
Procreate
An early attempt at pixel art where I focused on creating detail out of very little information. I wanted to create something extra-terrestrial with minimal detail, letting the atmosphere carry the mood.

Animation

"At Least it's Exercise"
Blender
This project was an experiment in lighting and atmosphere. I wanted to create a scene that felt unsettling, using lighting and shadows to emphasize movement and create a liminal-space.
(Based off of the following tutorial: https://youtu.be/imbIsNAvUpM)

Coding

"Online Portfolio"
HTML and CSS
This is the portfolio you are currently looking at. My goal is to make it easy to use and visually appealing. This has been a long-term project and is what got me interested in coding. I hope whoever is viewing it now appreciates the work and personal touches that have gone into it.

Physical Art

"Sign of the Times"
Woodworking and painting
This was made purely as a visual piece, combining painting and woodworking to create something eye-catching. I enjoyed the physical aspect of this and hope to do something similar in the future.

"Faceless Totem"
Wood Carving
Inspired by the faceless man from the movie "Spirited Away", I carved this to challenge myself and gain a new skill.

Music

"Spring Morning"
MPC Beats
A layered track inspired by hearing birds singing in the morning. The soft textures and slow build aim to capture the quiet calm feeling of waking up in a comforting cottage house without a care in the world.

"Stroll"
FL Studio
A Bossanova influenced piece meant to feel relaxed and easygoing. The chords and gentle groove make it a laid-back listen. My initial vision for this was to use it as a background for an animation, but I liked it too much as a standalone song.
